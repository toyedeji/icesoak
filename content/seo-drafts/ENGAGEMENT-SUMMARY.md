## Part 06 — On-Page SEO Standards
`icesoak-jul2026-part06-onpage-seo-standards.md`

Title tags: 50–60 chars, lead with keyword, `| IceSoak` suffix, formula per page type. Meta descriptions: 140–160 chars, include data point + CTA. One H1 per page, keyword-matched. URL patterns: lowercase hyphens, hierarchy-matched, no dates. Schema required by page type: LocalBusiness + GeoCoordinates (studio), BreadcrumbList (all), FAQPage (all with FAQ sections), ItemList (best-of), Article + dateModified (editorial), Review + Product (product pages). Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, INP < 200ms, CLS < 0.1. Images: WebP, explicit dimensions, descriptive alt text, < 200KB hero. Anchor text: descriptive, 3+ variations per destination. Canonicals: self-canonical on all pages, filter URLs canonical to base. 20-point pre-publish checklist.

## Part 10 — GEO/AI Search Optimization
`icesoak-jul2026-part10-geo-ai-search-optimization.md`

5 GEO principles: (1) Answer-first structure — 40–70 word quick-answer box on every page immediately below H1; (2) Entity-rich content — named businesses, neighborhoods, prices, temperatures rather than generic descriptions; (3) Structured data for AI — speakable schema on metro hubs and studio quick-facts, DefinedTerm schema for technical concepts (contrast therapy, cold plunge); (4) Freshness signals — dateModified in Article schema updated on every meaningful revision; (5) Citation-ready claims — specific, dateable, falsifiable facts AI models can attribute. Robots.txt: must NOT block GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot. Featured snippet targets: paragraph (definition queries), list (protocol queries), table (comparison queries). Monthly manual AI citation monitoring: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews for top 20 target queries.

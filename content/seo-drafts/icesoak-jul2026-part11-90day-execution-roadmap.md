## Purpose

This roadmap sequences every action from Parts 01–10 into a 90-day calendar. It assigns work to four functional tracks (Content, Technical, Links, Analytics), defines weekly targets, and sets the decision gates that determine whether to accelerate, hold, or pivot at each 30-day review.

## Month 1: Foundation (Days 1–30)
**Goal:** Close the most damaging technical and content gaps before adding new pages. A fast, well-structured site with 355 optimized pages is worth more than 400 pages with broken fundamentals.

---

### Week 1 (Days 1–7): Site Audit + Quick Wins

**Content Track**
- [ ] Audit all 11 metro hub pages against Part 06 Pre-Publish Checklist
  - Check title tags (formula compliance, character count)
  - Check meta descriptions (formula compliance, CTA present)
  - Check H1 (one per page, keyword-matched)
  - Check quick-answer box (present above fold, 40–70 words)
  - Log pass/fail per hub page in a tracking spreadsheet
- [ ] Fix any title tag or meta description failures immediately (highest CTR impact, easiest fix)
- [ ] Identify the 5 highest-rated studios per metro (sort by rating × review count)
  - Use IceSoak live directory data
  - These are the priority studio pages for schema audit in Week 2

**Technical Track**
- [ ] Test robots.txt — verify GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot are not blocked
- [ ] Verify sitemaps exist and are submitted to Google Search Console
- [ ] Run Core Web Vitals check on the 3 highest-traffic pages (likely root, 2 metro hubs)
- [ ] Check canonical tags on metro hub pages — ensure no filter parameter canonicalization issues

**Links Track**
- [ ] Pull Google Search Console Links report: document current inbound link count and top linking domains
- [ ] Identify top 30 listed studios with their own websites — these are the first studio partner link outreach targets

**Analytics Track**
- [ ] Confirm Google Search Console is connected and verified for icesoak.com
- [ ] Set up a rank tracking baseline for 15–20 target keywords (5 per priority metro: Denver, Seattle, Miami)
- [ ] Document current GSC data: total impressions, clicks, average position for `/cold-plunge/` and `/studio/` URL groups

**Deliverables by End of Week 1:**
- Metro hub audit spreadsheet (11 rows, pass/fail per checklist item)
- Robots.txt confirmed AI-crawler-friendly
- Rank baseline documented
- First studio partner outreach list (30 studios)

## Decision Gate Criteria

### At Day 30 (End of Month 1)
Proceed as planned if:
- At least 18 of 22 neighborhood pages are indexed in GSC
- Studio schema fixes are complete for 50+ studio pages
- No major technical issues (crawl errors, broken canonicals) found in initial audit

Adjust if:
- GSC shows crawl issues on new pages → prioritize technical track, delay content expansion
- Any metro hub page shows a significant ranking drop after optimization → review changes, revert if needed

### At Day 60 (End of Month 2)
Proceed to depth content if:
- At least 6 of 11 best-of roundups have indexed and are generating impressions
- At least 1 editorial backlink has been acquired
- GSC total clicks are trending up vs. Day 1

Accelerate affiliate content if:
- Best cold plunge tub page is generating affiliate clicks within 30 days of publication → invest more in Pillar 4
- Plateau if: total impressions are not increasing → diagnose whether indexing issues or keyword competition is the cause

### At Day 90 (End of Engagement)
See Part 12 KPI Dashboard for pass/fail thresholds.
If thresholds are met: proceed with second 90-day engagement, shifting focus to link building scale and Tier 3 neighborhood pages.
If thresholds are not met: conduct root cause analysis (technical issue? competitor response? wrong keyword targets?) before committing to next cycle.
